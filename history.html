History of Macaroni and Cheese

What do you think of when anyone says "Mac and Cheese?" Is it your grandmother's classic cheddar home recipe? Or your brother's gourmet twist with garlic breadcrumbs? Do you associate it with classic Americana cuisine or of another culture? Mac and Cheese is more than just a meal - it's a lifestyle. Here at Cheesology we understand that.
